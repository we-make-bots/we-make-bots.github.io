HOW WE WORK

THIS IS HOW YOUR BOT IS MADE!

IDEA

We get the ideas from our clients for the bots that they want us to make. Note you have to send a form response in order to tell us about your idea. This includes all features you want your bot to have and any specific details you want us to know about.

WORK

We then, put on our thinking caps and start working on the ideas. Just know that we are very experienced guys and will get your bot idea made. We might contact you while working, to check with any details about your bot. This time depends on the bot you want and the deadline you have given.

DELIVER

We then go through a thorough testing process for any bugs and finally deliver the bot to the client and recieve the payments. You are informed about the prices at the start of the project. We share the code of the bot and try to make our code user friendly, so that you can change / update it.
